## Our team

We are committed to building an inclusive, interdisciplinary forum that supports participation from early-career researchers and a broad international community. Our organizing team spans stellar spectroscopy, exoplanet interior modeling, and atmospheric retrieval — reflecting the cross-disciplinary spirit of the session itself.

Questions? Write to us at **diogosouto@academico.ufs.br**
